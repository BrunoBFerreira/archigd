procedure

(my-helper lst) → 
(listof
 (not/c (one-of/c 'cow)))
  lst : list?
Replaces each 'cow in lst with 'aardvark.

Examples:
> (my-helper '())

'()

> (my-helper '(cows such remarkable cows))

'(cows such remarkable cows)
